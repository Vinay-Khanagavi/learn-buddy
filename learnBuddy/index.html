Frequently asked questions

Here are a few questions answered to get to know Uicons better.

What makes Uicons special?

Uicons uses an icon grid that is twice the size of traditional icon sets, which use 24px.

Why is it significant? With a 48px grid, it’s possible to match the keylines and everything else to the golden ratio, resulting in more balanced and beautiful icons.

Other great reasons include:

  • Uicons have been rigorously tested and thoroughly produced to meet the highest quality standards.
  • Find icons instantly thanks to meticulously curated keywords.
  • Quickly copy and paste designer and developer-friendly SVGs into your designs or development environment with our user-friendly icon browser.
  • Easily customize color, strokes, and corner radius in Figma to adapt the icons to any project.

What can I do with this icon library?

The main styles (Round and Sharp) were designed to work very well in small sizes, around 24px, for all kinds of modern UIs, such as high-resolution websites and apps.

Especially the more unique styles like InkTrap, Cyber, Angled, and FluidPixel (included in Pro or available as individual sets) work also extraordinarily well in larger sizes as graphics or illustrations to give your designs and content a unique touch.

What’s included in the free version?

The free version includes 250 different icons in one style (Round Medium).

Show me all the free icons —>

How customizable are the icons?

All outline styles are highly customizable, and you can easily change stroke width, corner radius, color, and more in Figma. 

The solid styles are a bit less customizable because they are flattened in order to keep the complexity down.

Can I upgrade to a Pro Team or Enterprise plan?

You can upgrade to a Pro Team or Pro Enterprise plan at any time and will get a partial refund for your current plan. To upgrade please contact vinay.s.khanagavi@gmail.

When should you not use Uicons?

Uicons were designed on a 48px grid in favor of a very balanced and beautiful icon design aligned with the golden proportions for modern high-resolution screens.

More info on the grid —>

They have been tested and exported at 24px to work just as other icon sets. If you plan however to use them at 24px and still need to support low-resolution screens, they might not be the best option.

How can I receive updates?

New versions will be available on the download page in your account. As a subscriber you get optional updates about new releases and product improvements via email.

What are the future plans with Uicons?

The plan is to get to 600+ different icons soon and then to release icon and product updates on a regular basis further down the road.

Let me know if you want to see some particular icons in the next update -> vinay.s.khanagavi@gmail.

Feel free to email me at hi@uicons.com if you have further questions.
